Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Pakistan?

The best time to visit Pakistan depends on the region you plan to explore. Generally, spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) offer pleasant weather across most of the country. Northern areas are best visited from May to October, while southern regions are more comfortable during winter months (November to February).

Do I need a visa to visit Pakistan?

Yes, most foreign nationals require a visa to visit Pakistan. However, Pakistan has introduced an e-Visa system for many countries, making the process more convenient. We recommend checking the official Pakistan visa portal for the most up-to-date information.

Is Pakistan safe for tourists?

Pakistan's security situation has significantly improved in recent years, and many areas are now safe for tourism. Popular tourist destinations like Islamabad, Lahore, Hunza, and Skardu are generally very safe. However, we recommend checking travel advisories before planning your trip and considering a local guide for certain regions.
