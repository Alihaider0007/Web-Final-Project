Why Visit Pakistan

Breathtaking Landscapes

Home to five of the world's fourteen highest peaks, including K2, the second highest mountain on Earth.

Rich Heritage

Discover ancient civilizations, historic forts, and UNESCO World Heritage sites.

Culinary Delights

Savor the diverse and flavorful cuisine that varies from region to region.
